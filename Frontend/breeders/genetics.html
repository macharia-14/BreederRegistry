⚖️ Coefficient of Inbreeding (COI) Calculator
Wright's COI formula estimates the probability that an offspring will inherit two identical gene copies from a common ancestor, based on the recorded pedigree. Lower projected COI usually indicates lower inbreeding risk, but results can be underestimated when lineage records are incomplete.
